CCPA & GDPR Compliance

Introduction

At Resumify, we are committed to upholding your rights under data protection laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This section outlines how we collect, use, and manage your data in accordance with these regulations.

What is the CCPA?

The CCPA gives California residents the right to know what personal data we collect, how we use it, and how it is shared. It also provides the right to request access, deletion, or to opt out of the sale of personal data.

What is the GDPR?

The GDPR is a European regulation that gives users in the EU greater control over their personal data. It provides rights to access, correct, delete, and limit processing of your data, along with the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint.

Your Rights Under These Laws

  • The right to know what personal data is collected and how it is used.
  • The right to request access to your personal data.
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your data.
  • The right to opt-out of data sales (CCPA).
  • The right to withdraw consent or restrict processing (GDPR).
  • The right to data portability (GDPR).

How to Exercise Your Rights

You can request to access, delete, or modify your data by emailing us at privacy@resumify.com. We will verify your identity before processing any requests and respond within the legal timeframe required.

If you are a California resident, you may also use the “Do Not Sell or Share My Information” option available on our homepage.

Data Security and Retention

We apply strict security measures to protect your personal data and only retain it as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected. Your resume and personal details are never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
