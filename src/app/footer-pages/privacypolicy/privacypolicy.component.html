About Our App

Resumify is a powerful and intuitive web-based application designed to help individuals to manage and optimize the resumes. By leveraging advanced resume parsing technology, our platform extracts key details from uploaded resumes such as work experience, skills and education allowing for easier management and tailored improvements.

At Resumify we are committed to making the process of resume parsing and management as smooth as possible, while also ensuring that your personal information is protected and handled with care.
