Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I upload my resume?

To upload your resume, you need to be signed in to your account. Once logged in, click the “Upload Resume” button in the navigation bar. You can then select your PDF resume, and it will be parsed automatically.

2. What happens after I upload my resume?

Our system will extract key information such as your name, contact details, education, skills, and work experience. This information will be displayed in a clean, structured format for your review.

3. Do I need an account to use the resume upload feature?

Yes, only registered and logged-in users can upload resumes and access parsed resume data. This ensures your data is private and securely stored.

4. Can I edit or delete my uploaded resume details?

Currently, manual editing is not supported. However, you can delete your uploaded resume and upload a new version if needed. This feature is available in your Dashboard after logging in.

5. What file formats are supported for upload?

We currently support only PDF (.pdf) files for resume parsing. Make sure your resume is saved in a supported format before uploading.

6. Why can’t I log in to my account?

Double-check your email and password. If you’ve forgotten your password, click the “Forgot Password?” link on the login page to reset it. If you're new, make sure you've registered and activated your account through the confirmation email.

7. Does this platform help me build or check my resume?

No. This platform is focused on uploading and parsing resumes only. We do not currently offer resume creation or ATS scoring features.

8. Is my resume data safe?

Yes. Your uploaded resumes and parsed information are only visible to you after logging in. We follow standard data protection practices to keep your information secure.
