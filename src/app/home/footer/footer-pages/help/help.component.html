Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the Forgot Password page and enter your email address. You'll receive a link to create a new password.

 Can I edit my resume after downloading?

Yes! All your resumes are saved in your account. You can edit them anytime and download updated versions.

 How do I change my template?

Navigate to your resume editor and click "Change Template" in the top toolbar. You can preview all available templates before applying.
